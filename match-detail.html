Match Overview

The opening match of AFCON 2025 will see hosts Morocco take on Tanzania at the iconic Mohammed V Stadium in Casablanca. This Group A encounter promises to be an exciting start to the tournament, with both teams looking to make a strong statement early on.

Morocco, fresh from their historic World Cup semifinal appearance in 2022, will be looking to leverage home advantage and start their campaign with a win. Tanzania, making their third AFCON appearance, will be hoping to cause an upset against the tournament favorites.
