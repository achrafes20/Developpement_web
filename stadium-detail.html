Mohammed V Stadium

Casablanca, Morocco
Capacity: 45,000

Stadium Information

The Mohammed V Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in Casablanca, Morocco. It is used mostly for football matches and it also has athletics facilities. The stadium holds 45,000 spectators and was built in 1955.

The stadium underwent major renovations in 2024 in preparation for AFCON 2025. The upgrades included new seating, improved facilities for players and spectators, and state-of-the-art media and broadcasting equipment.

Key Features:

  • New hybrid pitch meeting FIFA standards
  • Modernized locker rooms and player facilities
  • Enhanced media center with broadcast capabilities
  • Improved accessibility for disabled spectators
  • Upgraded security and surveillance systems
  • New VIP and hospitality areas
