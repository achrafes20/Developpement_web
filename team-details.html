Team Overview

The Morocco national football team, nicknamed the Atlas Lions, represents Morocco in men's international football. The team is controlled by the Royal Moroccan Football Federation (FRMF).

Morocco won the Africa Cup of Nations in 1976 and were runners-up in 2004. They have qualified for the FIFA World Cup six times, with their best performance coming in 2022 when they became the first African team to reach the semifinals.

As hosts of AFCON 2025, Morocco will be looking to win their second continental title on home soil.
